Bringing Back the Blog

Published: Tue 22 December 2020
By Aaron Beals

In Blog.

Twenty-one years ago, I posted my first blog posts. For a while, I had a pretty good publishing streak going. But for the last several years, not only have I not been adding to my website -- I've completely let my website lapse.

I had a Wordpress site, but then it got hacked, so while I saved my content, I couldn't keep the site running. For a number of years, I had a flavors.me site with pointers to my Twitter account and LinkedIn profile, but then Flavors went under.

Setting up a blog isn't hard. But between being a father, husband, homeowner, and VP of Engineering, I have had my hands full. I made the decision to not prioritize writing, at least not in this forum.

But as 2020, a truly terrible year, wraps up, I decided it was time.
