About me

There are a few other folks named Aaron Beals on the web, so to clarify, I'm this one.

I've had a blog online in various forms since 1999, but you'll notice gaps in coverage due to life events.

Here, I write about various topics of personal, professional, and random interest. I mainly write for myself, as I've found writing helpful to focus my constantly-running mind in a world of information overload.
